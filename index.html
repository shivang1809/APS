Welcome to Army Public School, Jalandhar Cantt

From a modest beginning in 1971 as a Nursery school started by Mrs Sita Rawlley, the wife of Lt Gen HC Rawlley, PVSM, AVSM, MC , the then Corps Commander, the school has expanded its activities and assumed greater responsibilities. The school was named as Vajra Army School in Apr 1981. The school was upgraded to class X and affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi vide affiliation No 16205 on 01 May 1982....
